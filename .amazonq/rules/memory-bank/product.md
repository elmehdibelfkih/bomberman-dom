# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
Bomberman-DOM is a multiplayer web-based Bomberman game where multiple players can join battles until one remains as the last man standing. The game combines classic Bomberman gameplay mechanics with modern web technologies to deliver real-time multiplayer experiences.

## Key Features
- **Solo Play Mode**: Single-player campaign with multiple levels and progressive difficulty
- **Multiplayer Mode**: Real-time multiplayer battles supporting up to 4 players
- **Real-time Networking**: WebSocket-based communication for synchronized gameplay
- **Game Lobby System**: Player matchmaking with chat functionality and countdown timers
- **Progressive Levels**: Multiple maps with varying layouts and time constraints
- **Power-ups System**: Collectible items including speed boosts, extra time, and health
- **Audio/Visual Effects**: Comprehensive sound effects and explosion animations
- **Responsive Controls**: Keyboard-based movement and bomb placement
- **Game State Management**: Pause/resume, restart, and sound toggle functionality

## Target Users
- **Casual Gamers**: Players seeking nostalgic arcade-style gameplay
- **Multiplayer Enthusiasts**: Groups wanting competitive real-time battles
- **Web Game Players**: Users preferring browser-based games without installations
- **Retro Gaming Fans**: Players who enjoy classic Bomberman mechanics

## Use Cases
- **Local Multiplayer Sessions**: Friends playing together on the same network
- **Online Competitive Gaming**: Remote players joining battles from different locations
- **Solo Gaming Experience**: Individual players progressing through campaign levels
- **Party Gaming**: Group entertainment during social gatherings
- **Skill Development**: Players improving reaction time and strategic thinking
